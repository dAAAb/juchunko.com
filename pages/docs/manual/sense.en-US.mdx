# JC's Taste

## Digital Penmanship

- Ensure there is a "Bangkok's White" space before and after half-width English characters.
- Remember to format documents for JC.
- Always include subheadings in long texts.

## Content Editing

- When editing and pasting the content of letters, it must conform to the format or be without formatting.
- When showcasing screenshots in Word, be sure to hide the paragraph marks.
- Numbered lists must be formatted using tools, do not type manually.
- Indentation and layout must not use spaces!
